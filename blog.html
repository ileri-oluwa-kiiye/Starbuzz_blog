Starbuzz uses computer science

3/10/2012

Have you ever noticed how efficient a Starbuzz coffee house is?The lines always move fast, and despite the astronomical number of different drinks any customer can order, we have you drink up, hot(or cold if that's the way you want it) and ready in seconds. How do we do it?

To pull this off, we take advantage of the latest and greatest in computer science. In fact, we train our staff to be one big distibuted computer. The cashiers create the orders for the distributed computer, complete with your name and the drinks special instruction. Then our specialized drink makers grab the next cup and go about working on your order until it's finished.

With this design, we're able to horizontally scale our operation any time we want. All we need to do is add more cashiers and drink makers(not to mention a fair amount of support staff you never see) as the customer flow grows.
